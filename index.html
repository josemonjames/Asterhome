Our story

Built in Kakkanad, for families who can't always be there.

Aster was founded by a team with backgrounds in clinical nursing and hospital administration, after watching capable, loving families struggle with the same problem: an ageing parent at home, and children working in Bengaluru, Dubai, London or beyond.

We built Aster to be the reliable, well-trained presence a family would choose for their own parents — supervised care, honest communication, and no surprises.

What we do

A full range of home care services

Elderly & Senior Care

Daily living support — bathing, dressing, mobility, feeding — from caregivers trained in geriatric care.

Skilled Nursing Care

Registered nurses for wound care, injections, catheter and stoma care, and vitals monitoring at home.

Post-Hospital Recovery

Structured recovery plans after surgery, stroke or hospitalisation, coordinated with your doctor.

Companionship Care

A friendly, familiar presence for company, conversation, meals and light household support.

Palliative & Respite Care

Comfort-focused care for advanced illness, and short-term relief so family caregivers can rest.

Care Coordination Abroad

Video updates, expense tracking and a single point of contact for children living overseas.

How it works

STEP 01
